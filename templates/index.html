What

MeetingOne is a big scale event that PantherHackers has at the beginning of each Spring Semester.

Do not be deceived by the name! This is no ordinary meeting. MeetingOne is an opportunity which students that like technology can take advantage of to network with similar people, listen to all the great speakers we will have, and get food (YES. FREE FOOD). You will also get the chance to know PantherHackers, its leaders, what we have done for GSU, and what our plans for this semester and the future are in general.

Come learn how we can help you achieve your goals and maybe become part of our amazing family!
